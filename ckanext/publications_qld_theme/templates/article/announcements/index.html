    Queensland Government Publications web address changes

    Due to department changes, some Queensland Government web addresses have been updated to their new department names.

    If you have bookmarked links, these may no longer work and will need to be updated.

    To find publication sets, you can enter keywords in the search bar or use the left menu to search for the publication by category, organisation, or tags.

    The departments are listed on the organisations page, the changes are sumarised below.

    Department name changes
    From To
    Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander and Multicultural Affairs Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Partnerships
    Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry Agriculture and Fisheries
    Education, Training and Employment Education and Training
    Local Government, Community Recovery and Resilience Infrastructure, Local Government and Planning
    National Parks, Recreation, Sport and Racing National Parks, Sport and Racing
    Queensland Treasury and Trade Queensland Treasury
    Science, Information Technology, Innovation and the Arts Science, Information Technology and Innovation
